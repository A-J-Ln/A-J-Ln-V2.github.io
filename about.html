BRAND STORY

Established in 2025 by Alicia J Lin, CRYBABY CO., Ltd. Is a Canadian coffee company born from bruises and tears. Inspired by those who always get back up when life pushes them down, CRYBABY takes pride in their quiet rebellion. We don’t believe in pretending everything’s okey. We believe in facing the day anyway. At the heart of our brand is DevilBaby - a boy in a devil-hood. He’s been through a lot and has the bruises to show for it. Representing the core of what CRYBABY stands for, DevilBaby cries without apology. Because sometimes, strength is found in the tears we don’t hide. Some days, you need armour. Other days, you just need coffee. CRYBABY is here for both.
